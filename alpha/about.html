About Alpha Consultancy

Alpha Consultancy

Premium Bespoke Consulting

Alpha Consultancy was founded in 2020 by Lena Jung, a leading consultant with over 20 years of experience in U.S. and U.K. boarding school admissions, together with a team of specialists in various educational fields.

Each year, we successfully place students into top boarding and junior boarding schools while guiding their personal and academic growth beyond admission.

Working closely with educational consultants and guardians in the United States, Alpha Consultancy is committed to achieving the best possible outcomes for both students and parents.

매년 최상위 보딩스쿨 및 주니어 보딩스쿨에 학생들을 성공적으로 입학시키며, 합격 이후에도 학생들의 개인적 성장과 학업적 발전을 지속적으로 지원합니다.

Process

The Alpha Process

01

Consultation

Post-Management
